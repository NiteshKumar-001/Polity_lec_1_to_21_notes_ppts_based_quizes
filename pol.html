Correct: B. Remission means reducing the duration (period) of the sentence without changing its character.
169. What does the pardoning power of 'Respite' specifically apply to?
Correct: C. Respite denotes awarding a lesser sentence in place of one originally awarded due to some special fact (e.g., pregnancy of a woman offender).

Correct: D. Reprieve means a stay of the execution of a sentence for a temporary period.
171. What is the fundamental difference between the pardoning powers of the President and the Governor of a state?

Correct: D. Article 267 allows the Parliament to establish a Contingency Fund for meeting unforeseen expenditure pending authorization by Parliament.

Correct: C. Article 148 establishes the CAG as the auditor-general who ensures financial accountability.

Correct: D. The CAG submits reports to the President, who then causes them to be laid before each House of Parliament.
